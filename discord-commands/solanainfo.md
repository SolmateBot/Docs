# /solanainfo

Display global Solana blockchain information at a glance.

## Usage

`/solanainfo`

No options required.

## What it shows

* Total supply, circulating supply, and non-circulating supply
* Current inflation rate
* Staking APY
* Total transaction count
* Daily active users
* Daily fees and rewards
